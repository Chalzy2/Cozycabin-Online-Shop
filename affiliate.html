How Affiliate Works

Earn on every referral sale

›

1️⃣ Register for free — takes 30 seconds.
2️⃣ Get your unique link like cozycabin.co.ke/?ref=CZ-XXXX
3️⃣ Share the link on WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, anywhere.
4️⃣ When someone buys through your link, you earn a commission automatically.
5️⃣ Withdraw your earnings via MiniPay, M-Pesa or PayPal.

💬

WhatsApp Marketing Tips

Best ways to share on WhatsApp

›

✅ Post your link in WhatsApp Groups (community, family, work).
✅ Use your WhatsApp Status — it's free advertising to all your contacts.
✅ Send personalized messages: "Hey, found great solar lights at Cozycabin — nationwide delivery!"
✅ Share product images with your link in the caption.
✅ Create a WhatsApp Broadcast list for regular customers.
✅ Always include your affiliate link — even in casual chats!

📘

Facebook Marketing Tips

Reach thousands for free

›

✅ Post in Facebook Groups (Buy & Sell, your town's group, solar fans).
✅ Share product photos with your affiliate link in the post.
✅ Create a Facebook Page for your shop and post daily.
✅ Use Facebook Stories — they appear at the top for all your friends.
✅ Tag friends who might be interested in the product.
✅ Reply to every comment — engagement boosts your post reach for free.

How Commissions Work

How you get paid per sale

›

💡 When a customer buys through your link, your balance is automatically updated.

📊 Commission rates vary by product category.
⏱️ Commissions are confirmed after the customer receives their order.
🔁 Returning customers who use your link keep earning you commission.
📲 You can track every click and sale from your dashboard.
💸 Once you reach KES 500, you can request a withdrawal anytime.

💸

How to Withdraw

Get your money fast

›

1️⃣ Make sure your balance is KES 500 or more.
2️⃣ Go to Withdraw in the bottom menu.
3️⃣ Enter the amount and choose your payment method:
   ⭐ MiniPay — fastest, recommended
   📱 M-Pesa — sent to your number
   💙 PayPal — for international
4️⃣ Click Request Withdrawal.
5️⃣ Admin reviews and pays within 24–48 hours.
